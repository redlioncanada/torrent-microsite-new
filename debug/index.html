BERRY SOY-CREAM BLEND

INGREDIENTS

Makes 2 servings

2

cups frozen mixed berries

14

ounces blackberries with juice or 1 cup fresh blackberries

1

cup soy milk or almond milk

1

cup apple juice

1

cup soft tofu (4 ounces)

INSTRUCTIONS

1

Place frozen berries, blackberries, soy milk, apple juice and tofu in pitcher of KitchenAid® Torrent™ Blender. Cover and blend on Icy Drinks/Smoothies setting.

2

Pour into two glasses; serve immediately.

MAGNETIC DRIVE

1.8 PEAK HP*. 18,000 RPMS.

The Magnetic Drive system securely locks the pitcher in place during operation. Forty-eight permanent magnets connect the pitcher to the base, transferring power from the motor into the pitcher. Unlike other blenders, the pitcher slides in and out like a coffee maker, so there's no need to move it out from underneath kitchen cabinets, twist the pitcher into place, or hold down the lid during blending.
